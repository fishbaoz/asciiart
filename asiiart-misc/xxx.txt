kinda 'sexual game summery'.  Unfortunately for most of you, its in
german and maybe someone will translate it, after I finished and
post it.  Since I tried hard on these few lines, I've got the clue,

Because that text is (in the current state) for a special word
processing program and has grown rather huge, we think about right-
sizing and converting it to a plain ascii text file so everyone
can use it without problems.

After I stumbled upon 'alt.ascii-art', the idea was born.

So, 'Gina' and I spent much time of one rainy weekend creating one
pix after another. Watching me squeezing a bunch of tiny drawings
through the keyboard up to the screen, 'Gina' requested me, to
make an extract of these for alt.ascii-art.  I put some additional
work onto it and finally I think that she was right.  Keep in mind
that the most of the pix are primarily designed as illustrations
to loosen up the pages, where space was limited. The few bigger
ones are new creations (perhaps I include some of them in my
game summary).

Since two weekends didn't turn a newbie a into master of ascii
art and since I didn't take much care to different fonts, country
modes, keyboard layouts (mine is a german one) and that stuff,
there is a real chance that some chars may become screwed up.
